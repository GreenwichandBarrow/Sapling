## HOW SHE COULD BE USEFUL TO YOUR SEARCH

1. **Deep art world network** from 20+ years at Christie's, Sotheby's, and now Rauschenberg Foundation. Likely knows every major player in art services, storage, insurance, logistics.
2. **Strategic initiatives role** means she works with vendors and service providers across the art ecosystem. Direct line to companies G&B might evaluate.
3. **Foundation world connections.** Art foundations use specialized services (conservation, storage, documentation, insurance) that overlap with G&B's thesis areas.
4. **Potential river guide** for the art insurance brokerage niche and any art-adjacent services.

## WHAT TO SHARE ABOUT YOURSELF

- Kate already knows about your Chanel background and that you're starting "a new venture that intersects with the art world" (Karaugh's framing)
- Build on this. Talk about your transition from luxury to entrepreneurship and your interest in the infrastructure behind the art world, not just the art itself.
- Keep G&B positioning conversational. You're exploring businesses that serve the art world behind the scenes. No need to say "acquisition" or "fund."
- Your shared world: You both come from the high-end art market. You understand the ecosystem she operates in. That's your credibility.

---

## WHAT NOT TO OVER-SHARE

- Don't go deep on specific acquisition targets or niches. Keep it broad: "art-related services."
- Don't mention specific companies you're evaluating.
- Don't talk about fundraising, investors, or fund structure.
- This is a relationship-first coffee. Let it breathe. If she can be useful, it will emerge naturally.

---

## SUGGESTED TALKING POINTS

1. **The Rauschenberg Centennial.** Ask about the Jason Wu collaboration and what it's like to steward an artist's legacy at this scale. She's clearly proud of this work.
2. **Her transition from Sotheby's to the foundation world.** What drew her to it? What's different about working at a foundation vs. an auction house?
3. **The infrastructure behind foundations.** What vendors and service providers does the foundation rely on? (Storage, conservation, insurance, documentation, shipping.)
4. **Your exploration.** "I've been looking into the businesses that keep the art world running behind the scenes. I'd love your perspective on what that landscape looks like from where you sit."
