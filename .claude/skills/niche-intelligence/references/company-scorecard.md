# G&B Company Scorecard

Source: `G&B Industry & Company Scorecard Template.xlsx` (TEMPLATE tab, lower section)
Location: MANAGER DOCUMENTS / G&B MASTER TEMPLATES
Local copy: `brain/library/internal/scorecard/G&B Industry & Company Scorecard Template.xlsx`

**This is the canonical company-level scoring template.** Used when evaluating individual acquisition targets — conference attendees, Linkt-sourced companies, broker deals.

## 10 Criteria, Weighted, Score 1-10

| # | Criterion | Weight | + (Best) | +/- (Mid) | - (Worst) |
|---|-----------|--------|----------|-----------|-----------|
| 1 | Clear Value Proposition & Mission Criticality | 15% | Clear value proposition; mission critical for primary ICP | Partial clarity; benefits visible but ROI not always evident | Unclear value or non-mission-critical; easily delayed or cut |
| 2 | Market is Hospitable (& Sizeable) to Grow | 15% | 35%+ penetration; strong tailwinds | Growing strong in smaller segments | Slower growth in larger/target segments |
| 3 | Company has a 'Right to Win' at Attractive ICP | 10% | Wins frequently; strong fit | Needs brand elevation or credibility | Poor ICP fit |
| 4 | Company Retains Its Customers | 10% | 100% gross / 110% net retention | 95% gross (industry average) | Usage decreases or churn risk |
| 5 | Revenue Concentration | 10% | Low customer concentration; diversified | 1-2 customers = 20-30% | High concentration >40% |
| 6 | Revenue Predictability (transformation opportunity) | 10% | Partially recurring today, with clear opportunity to convert to fully recurring | Already largely recurring (80-90%), limiting transformation upside | Fully non-recurring or lumpy project-based; no path to predictability |
| 7 | Unit Economics are Attractive; Sizeable Co. | 10% | High gross margins; >$5M revenue | Moderate EBITDA / growing margins | Small business; low margins |
| 8 | Technology is Sound; Product is Scalable | 5% | Cloud-native, scalable | Needs modernization | Legacy / unscalable |
| 9 | Valuation | 5% | Low absolute price (~$10M) or attractive multiple | Reasonable (~3.5x Revenue) | High multiple (~6x ARR) / premium paid |
| 10 | Strong Team, Seller Willingness, & Smooth Transition | 10% | Seller ready; strong team | Needs transition or talent adds | Seller unwilling or team weak |

**Total: 100% weighted score on 1-10 scale**

## Key Design Notes

- **Criterion 6 (Revenue Predictability)** scores highest for companies with conversion opportunity, NOT companies already fully recurring. This is a value-creation lens — the upside is in converting non-recurring to recurring.
- **Criterion 8 (Technology)** has lowest weight (5%) — reflects that most targets are services businesses, not software.
- **Criterion 9 (Valuation)** also 5% — this is a screening filter, not the primary decision driver.
- **Criteria 1-2 carry 30% combined** — mission criticality and market growth are the most important factors.

## When to Use (Situational, Not Universal)

The scorecard adds value when differentiating within a large pool or making go/no-go decisions. It's overhead on small daily batches where buy box hard gates already filter.

| Situation | Score? | Why |
|-----------|--------|-----|
| Conference attendee list (50-200 people) | YES — quick partial score | Need to prioritize a large pool for pre-conference outreach (top 10) |
| Linkt daily batch (4-6 targets) | NO — buy box hard gates are enough | Every approved target gets outreach anyway |
| Deal progresses to financials received | YES — full score with real data | Structured evaluation for go/no-go decision |
| Sprint check-in (2-week ICP review) | YES — score a sample retrospectively | Calibrate whether ICP is producing the right companies |

## Distinction from Industry Scorecard

- **Industry Scorecard** (9 criteria, 30 pts) — evaluates the niche/industry as a whole. Used by niche-intelligence.
- **Company Scorecard** (10 criteria, 1-10 scale) — evaluates a specific company within a niche. Used by target-discovery, conference-discovery, and deal-intake.

Both live on the same Excel template (TEMPLATE tab) but serve different purposes at different stages of the funnel.
