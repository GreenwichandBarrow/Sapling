- [ ] **Cod w/ string beans & yuca fries**
  > ## **Simple Lemon-Garlic Cod with String Beans**
  > 
  > *(Yuca fries reheated on the side)*
  > 
  > ### **Ingredients**
  > 
  > - Cod fillets
  > - Olive oil
  > - Salt & black pepper
  > - Garlic (1–2 cloves, or garlic powder)
  > - Lemon (or lemon juice)
  > - Butter (optional but recommended)
  > - String beans
  > - Optional finish: parsley, dill, or a squeeze of lemon
  > 
  > ---
  > 
  > ## **Step 1: Reheat the yuca fries (hands-off)**
  > 
  > **Oven method (best texture):**
  > 
  > - Oven to **400°F**
  > - Spread yuca fries on a sheet pan
  > - Bake **12–15 minutes**, flipping once if needed
  >     → They can stay warm while you cook the fish
  > 
  > ---
  > 
  > ## **Step 2: Blister the string beans**
  > 
  > 1. Heat a wide pan over **medium-high**
  > 2. Add **olive oil**
  > 3. Add string beans in a single layer
  > 4. Let them sit **undisturbed 2–3 minutes** to get a little char
  > 5. Stir, season with **salt + pepper**
  > 6. Add a tiny splash of water, cover **1–2 minutes** to finish cooking
  > 7. Optional: finish with lemon or a small pat of butter
  > 
  > Remove to a plate and keep warm.
  > 
  > ---
  > 
  > ## **Step 3: Cook the cod (simple + juicy)**
  > 
  > Use the **same pan** (wipe if needed).
  > 
  > 1. Lower heat to **medium**
  > 2. Add olive oil + small pat of butter
  > 3. Season cod with **salt & pepper**
  > 4. Place cod in pan (presentation side down first)
  > 5. Cook **3–4 minutes**, don’t move it
  > 6. Flip gently, cook **2–3 minutes more**
  > 7. Add garlic for the last 30 seconds (so it doesn’t burn)
  > 8. Finish with **lemon squeeze**
  > 
  > Cod should flake easily but still be moist.
  > 
  > ---
  > 
  > ## **Optional quick sauce (30 seconds)**
  > 
  > If you want a little something:
  > 
  > - Deglaze pan with **1–2 Tbsp lemon juice**
  > - Swirl in **tiny knob of butter**
  > - Spoon over fish
  > 
  > ---
  > 
  > ## **Timing (total ~25 minutes)**
  > 
  > - Yuca fries: 12–15 min (oven)
  > - String beans: 6–8 min
  > - Cod: 6–7 min
