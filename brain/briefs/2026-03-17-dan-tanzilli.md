## What to Share About Yourself

- **Chanel background** — he already knows this. Lean into it as shared luxury/brand DNA.
- **Greenwich & Barrow** — we acquire small B2B businesses, particularly in luxury-adjacent and niche services.

## What Not to Over-Share

- Don't lead with the analytical side — keep it conversational, relationship-first.

1. **His business** — how Third Eye has evolved since 2014, what the art/design PR landscape looks like now.
2. **Art storage/logistics** — does he see pain points in how clients store, ship, or insure art? Any businesses doing it well or poorly?
3. **Your search** — briefly share what G&B does, see if anything in his world sparks a connection.
4. **Mutual value** — what would be helpful for him? Relationship-first.
