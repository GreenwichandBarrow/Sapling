1. **Outsourced outreach at scale.** DealsX scrapes 30+ databases and runs personalized multi-channel campaigns (email + LinkedIn). This could replace your current stack of JJ (VA cold calling) + Linkt (list building) + Claude (email drafting) with a single provider that handles discovery, enrichment, and outreach autonomously.
2. **Email deliverability expertise.** One testimonial specifically highlights Sam's command of email deliverability and staying ahead of algorithm changes. This is a real pain point for cold outreach, as your sender domain reputation is critical.
3. **Search fund experience.** DealsX works with search funds specifically, not just PE firms with different deal sizes. Sam should understand the search fund volume, deal size, and thesis constraints.
4. **Budget-neutral swap potential.** JJ costs $1,040/month, Linkt Pro was $300/month, Google Voice $10/month. If DealsX pricing is in the $1,000-1,500 range, this is roughly budget-neutral while potentially increasing outreach quality and autonomy.

## SUGGESTED TALKING POINTS

1. **How DealsX works.** "Walk me through what happens after I sign up. What does the first 30 days look like?" Get the specifics: do they build the target list, or do you provide it? How do they personalize outreach? What does reporting look like?
2. **List building vs. outreach.** "Do you handle the company identification and list building, or are you primarily running outreach against lists I provide?" This is a critical distinction. You need someone who can find companies, not just email them.
3. **Cold calling.** "Does your service include phone outreach, or is it email and LinkedIn only?" If email/LinkedIn only, you may still need a calling function.
4. **Search fund references.** "Can you connect me with 2-3 search fund clients I could speak with?" Independent references are essential. Megan's experience is promising but early.
5. **Pricing and terms.** "What does engagement look like? Monthly fee, success fee, contract length?" Get specific numbers. Compare against JJ ($1,040/month) and CAPTARGET ($1,500-3,000/month, no success fee).
6. **The $200M claim.** "You mention $200M in deal value uncovered. Can you break that down? How many deals closed vs. intros made vs. meetings set?" Understand what "uncovered" actually means.
7. **Deliverability.** "How do you handle email deliverability? Do you send from my domain or yours?" This matters for your sender reputation.
