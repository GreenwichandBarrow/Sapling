# Women's Search Network: How to Set Up for a Search

**Date:** 2025-04-24
**Attendees:** Adair Sloan, Elise Testa, Hailey Hu, Hannah Barrett, Iris Li, Jean Guo, Kaylyn Kavi, Lynda Qin
**Duration:** 63 minutes

---

## Notes

The Women's Search Network panel discussion, co-hosted by Hannah Barrett and Kaylyn Kavi, focused on strategies for effectively launching a business search, with insights from recent graduates Jean Guo, Elise Testa, and Iris Li—all of whom began their searches in fall 2023. The panel highlighted critical tools for search setup, emphasizing the importance of databases like Grata and Source Scrub for deal sourcing, as well as effective email outreach through platforms such as Reply IO and Apollo IO. They underscored the necessity of early domain warming for better email deliverability and the management of interns through structured training. The discussion also covered early decision-making strategies, successful thesis generation, and the significance of community support in location choices, aiming to mitigate isolation during the search process. The session concluded with a Q&A on network building and fundraising, stressing the importance of establishing relationships with investors and building a network of industry advisors for valuable introductions. Action items included early acquisition of email domains, leveraging business school resources, and maximizing FDIC insurance coverage through multiple bank accounts.
