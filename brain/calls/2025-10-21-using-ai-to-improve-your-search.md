# Using AI to Improve your Search

**Date:** 2025-10-21
**Attendees:** Dino Beslic, GJ King, Grace Chen, Harrison Wells, Luke, Ryan Robinson, lucas braun
**Duration:** 61 minutes

---

## Notes

- AI tools like ChatGPT Deep Research can generate comprehensive reports in 10–15 minutes by sourcing 60–70 references, ideal for initial market intelligence despite limitations with private data access.
- Claude Code distinguishes itself by deploying 10+ subagents for tailored research, able to scrape walled-off sources, improving intelligence quality significantly for acquisition strategies.
- The live demo on the fire sprinkler industry underlined Claude's ability to combine public and proprietary data, yielding precise market insights and enhancing deal origination.
- Best practices encourage using subagents for complex tasks, increasing precision and reducing data hallucination risks compared to single-prompt systems.
- Platforms like Linked automate list building based on detailed Ideal Customer Profiles, focusing on specific firmographics, which supports higher quality lead generation.
- Iterative refinement of Ideal Customer Profiles demonstrated that defined targeting criteria directly affects the accuracy and relevance of lead lists.
- Weekly searches targeting highly filtered companies were recommended as a phased approach to efficient resource use and increased engagement.
- Automation tools like Phantom Buster streamline social media engagement, enhancing content reach and lead generation aligned with social selling strategies.
- Niche AI tools focused on specific use cases, such as Granola for call transcription, were advised for more impactful and high-quality user experiences.
- A tiered approach for tool adoption recommended starting with simpler solutions before progressing to advanced platforms like Claude Code to optimize research outcomes.
