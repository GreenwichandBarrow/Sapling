# Axial

## Quick Facts
- LMM M&A deal network — ~5,000 active buyers, deals typically $10M-$50M EV
- Facilitated 340 owner introductions to members last year
- Sweet spot: $1-5M EBITDA, with competition concentrated in $1-3M band
- Free for sell-side brokers; success-fee-only tier for qualified buyers
- Publishes free industry data + league tables + quarterly industry reports at axial.net

## Communication Style
- **Tone:** Data-driven, market-commentary oriented
- **Format:** Panel talks, written reports, deck-based member briefings
- **Frequency:** Quarterly industry reports; weekly deal flow on platform
