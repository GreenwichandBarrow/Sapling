# BankUnited

## Quick Facts
- National bank, HQ Miami Lakes FL; NY metro presence + Dallas TX
- Public (NYSE: BKU), ~$36B assets, mid-cap commercial bank
- Specialized middle-market lending groups: Healthcare, ESOP, NDFI (non-depository financial institutions), Structured Finance, Corporate Loan Syndications, Public/Project Finance, Trade Finance, Non-Profit, Government, ESG
- SBA Preferred Lender — up to 80% financing on business acquisitions, 10-year terms, no prepayment penalty
