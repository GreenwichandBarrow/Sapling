- Vertical SaaS: CRM and ERP for chemical distribution and process manufacturing
- Currently valued at ~$1B (no exit yet)
- Founded 1981, Florham Park NJ, 51-250 employees, estimated ARR $50-100M
- Founded via search fund acquisition — acquired by searcher, built into platform
