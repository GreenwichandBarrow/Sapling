# STREAM Capital Partners, LLC

## Quick Facts
- National real estate advisory firm founded 2012, headquartered in Chicago, IL
- Specialty: net-lease investment sales and sale-leaseback advisory
- Asset classes: retail, industrial, office, medical office, special-purpose
- Transaction volume cited: $3B+ since inception
- ~34 employees per third-party sources

## What They Do
- Represent buyers and sellers of single-tenant net-leased commercial properties nationally.
- Structure and execute sale-leasebacks for companies that own real estate and want to unlock value.
- Client base spans private equity sponsors, family offices, institutional investors, and owner-occupiers.
- "International buyer platform" + proprietary distribution database.
