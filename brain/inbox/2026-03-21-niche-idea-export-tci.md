## Niche Idea: Export Trade Credit Insurance Brokerage

**Source:** Split from Trade Credit Insurance niche during March 21 testing. Kay identified that domestic and export TCI are different niches with different customers, carriers, and risk profiles.

**What it is:** Specialty brokers who place export credit insurance for US exporters to protect against foreign buyer non-payment and political risk. Tied to EXIM Bank, FCIA/Great American programs.

**How it differs from Domestic TCI:**
- Clients: US exporters (vs. domestic B2B)
- Risk: geopolitical + sovereign (vs. commercial credit)
- Carriers: EXIM, FCIA, plus commercial carriers
- Often bundled with political risk insurance
- More complex underwriting

**Named companies already identified:**
- Impello Global (Seattle, EXIM Broker of Year 2023)
- Export Risk Management Inc. (Salisbury CT)
- ACI Broker Group (East Aurora NY)
- Several others from FCIA list

**Initial fit assessment:** Passes initial screen (recurring, compliance-driven, asset-light). Needs separate target pool validation and one-pager. Kay wants to focus on Domestic TCI first but this is worth evaluating through the pipeline later.
