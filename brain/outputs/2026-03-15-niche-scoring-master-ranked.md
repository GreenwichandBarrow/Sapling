# G&B Luxury Infrastructure Niche Scoring: Master Ranked List

Pre-scored 150+ niches across 15 luxury verticals against G&B scorecard criteria. Scored by AI agents with web research on 2026-03-15. Scores normalized to /24 (8 criteria x 3 max each).

## Scoring Criteria (1-3 each)
1. **Growth** — relative to GDP
2. **Margins** — EBITDA
3. **Recurring Revenue** — % recurring
4. **Fragmentation** — number of players
5. **Mission Criticality** — switching costs
6. **Tech Disruption** — risk level (3 = low risk)
7. **Right to Win** — luxury background advantage
8. **Acquirability** — retirement-age owner-operators

## STRATEGIC CLUSTERS

Three high-conviction themes emerge across all verticals:

### Cluster 1: UHNW Property & Estate Management
**Niches:** Estate management companies, multi-property management, home watch, seasonal management, luxury residence concierge
**Thesis:** The "operating system" for UHNW real estate portfolios. Recurring monthly retainers, extreme fragmentation in wealthy enclaves (Hamptons, Palm Beach, Aspen, Greenwich), retirement-age owners, luxury background is the ideal credential.
**Roll-up play:** Acquire in one market, expand to adjacent UHNW geographies.
**Alignment with Q3 2025 thesis:** This IS your "Concierge Home Management Services" vertical, validated with data.

### Cluster 2: UHNW Asset Infrastructure
**Niches:** Aircraft/yacht management, aviation/yacht insurance brokerage, specialized accounting, art storage, watch servicing, car storage
**Thesis:** Recurring management/insurance/service fees on UHNW hard assets. High switching costs (trust + asset risk). Fragmented with aging owner-operators. Multi-niche roll-up within one asset class creates a "family office for [asset type]."
**Roll-up play:** Aircraft management + aviation insurance + aviation CPA = full-service aviation family office.

### Cluster 3: UHNW Family Infrastructure
**Niches:** Trust administration, family governance, travel coordination, private staff management, admissions consulting, estate valuation
**Thesis:** Services the family behind the wealth — governance, lifestyle, education, succession. Growing with $84T wealth transfer. Advisory margins, retainer-based, relationship-driven.
**Roll-up play:** Family governance + travel coordination + staff management = integrated family office services platform.
