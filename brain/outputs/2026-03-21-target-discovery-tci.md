## Key Research Findings

### FCIA Broker Directory is the Master Source
The FCIA (Great American) authorized broker listing yielded 31 brokers total. After removing large brokerages (Marsh, Aon, WTW, Gallagher, Lockton, Hub, NFP, Brown & Brown, Marsh MMA), acquired firms (Texel, GCC), and the Alliant/One Source division, **15-17 firms remain as potential independent targets**. This aligns with the vault's prior estimate of 20-50 US TCI specialists.

### EXIM Broker Locator Validates Quality
EXIM's platinum tier (highest designation) includes ARI Global, Trade Acceptance Group, and Securitas Global -- all on our Active list. EXIM broker status is a quality signal for TCI specialization.

### ICBA Network is Small in US
Only 2 US-based ICBA members (Impello Global in WA, James Goodliffe in TX). The network is more European-focused. US TCI brokerage is a small community.

### Succession Signals
- **EIA Global** (Albert Giandomenico, founded 1979) -- strongest succession signal
- **Credit Insurance International** (Joel Berman, in TCI since 1972) -- strong
- **Trade Acceptance Group** -- unknown, need to identify owner vs. operations staff
- **Securitas Global** (co-founders with 35+ years experience) -- moderate

### Recent M&A Activity
- **GCC acquired by WTW** (April 2025) -- validates that TCI brokers ARE acquisition targets
- **Meridian acquired by Texel** (completed full acquisition) -- validates international interest
- **Impello acquired JZJ Insurance** -- shows even small TCI firms are doing M&A
- **Amynta acquired Crum & Forster credit division** (Dec 2024) -- MGA consolidation
