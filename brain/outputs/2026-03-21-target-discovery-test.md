## Key Observations

1. **NEIS is the marquee target.** Family-owned since 1945, does both premium audit and loss control, nationwide coverage via partners in 28 states. Located in Cheshire, CT (not Davenport, IA as originally noted). Need to identify owner/leadership.

2. **Contact gaps are significant.** Most companies found via web research lack owner email/phone. Linkt enrichment would be valuable here once credits are available. Consider using individual Linkt enrichment on the top 5-6 targets.

3. **Consolidation is already happening.** Three companies from the overnight named list were recently acquired (Cox & Smith, Lowry, Afirm). This validates the thesis that this niche is attractive to acquirers. Brightstone, ReSource Pro, and Davies are all actively consolidating.

4. **Two trade associations identified:**
   - **NSIPA** (National Society of Insurance Premium Auditors) - vendor directory with member companies
   - **ARCO** (Associated Reporting Companies, est. 1967) - trade association of independent insurance inspection/audit companies. Member directory at arco-members.com.

5. **Luque is small but interesting.** California-only, family-run (three Luques in leadership). May be too small. Worth a look.

6. **IPI (Hopkins, MN) is substantial.** 430+ employees, 32 states. Founded 1996 by current owner. Could be near the upper end of target size.

7. **SCI Inspections** has $28M revenue and only 32 employees. That revenue-to-headcount ratio suggests heavy use of 1099 contractors (400+ nationwide). Interesting model.
