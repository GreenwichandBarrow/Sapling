## Diagnostic Questions (8)

Questions derived from niche data: $4.78B market growing 6.4% CAGR, 1,200+ firms globally, 79% of wealth stakeholders want art integrated into wealth services (Deloitte 2025).

1. How did you find/select your art advisor?
2. Retainer-based, project-based, or commission model?
3. How long with current advisor, ever switched?
4. What services do you value most?
5. Gaps between what advisor provides and what you need?
6. Consolidation trend or still mostly small independents?
7. Trust/taste alignment vs operational capability?
8. Is art being integrated into wealth services in practice?

## Validation Targets

Key risks to validate from niche data:
- Whether advisory relationships are truly sticky or project-based
- Whether wealth management firms are building in-house advisory (displacing independents)
- Whether 1,200+ firms globally means oversupply or healthy fragmentation
- Whether UHNW families pay retainers or only commission on transactions
