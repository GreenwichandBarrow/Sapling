## Diagnostic Questions (8)

Questions derived from niche scoring: scored 20/24, recurring management fees, high switching costs, fragmented with aging practitioners.

1. Ever hired an external collection management consultant? What prompted it?
2. How did you find the consultant? (ARCS, AAM, referral, search?)
3. What specific services did they provide?
4. How critical is CMS platform expertise (TMS, PastPerfect, Axiell)?
5. Biggest unmet need in collection management you wish you could outsource?
6. When budgets are tight, is collection management consulting cut first or kept?
7. Is demand growing with digitization and staffing challenges?
8. What would make you pay a premium?

## Validation Targets

Key risks to validate from niche data:
- Whether museums/galleries actually outsource or only use in-house staff
- Whether budget constraints create real demand or just kill the market
- Whether digitization is a one-time project or creates ongoing consulting need
- Whether corporate collections are a meaningful customer segment or too small
