## Diagnostic Questions (8)

Questions derived from one-pager risks: carrier concentration (Big 3 control 85%), US underpenetration (2-5% vs 10-15% Europe), trade volatility tailwinds, and broker value-add beyond annual placement.

1. How did you originally find your TCI broker?
2. How long with current broker, ever switched?
3. If broker disappeared, how easy to replace?
4. Does your broker give real leverage with Big 3 carriers?
5. What does broker do beyond annual placement?
6. Has tariff/trade volatility changed your TCI coverage?
7. What would you pay more for?
8. Other companies that should buy TCI but don't?

## Validation Targets

Key risks to validate from one-pager:
- Whether broker relationships are truly sticky (claimed 85-95% retention)
- Whether Big 3 carrier dominance undermines broker value
- Whether US market underpenetration is awareness problem or product problem
- Whether mid-market companies are increasingly adopting TCI
