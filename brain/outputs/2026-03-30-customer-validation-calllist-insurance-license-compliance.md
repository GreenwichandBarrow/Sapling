## Diagnostic Questions (8)

Questions derived from one-pager risks: technology disruption (AgentSync, Agenzee), PE consolidation narrowing target pool, high switching costs, and regulatory moat strength.

1. In-house, outsourced, or combination? What drove the decision?
2. How many active licenses across how many states?
3. How did you find your compliance provider?
4. Biggest pain point in multi-state licensing?
5. Ever had a compliance lapse cause operational disruption?
6. Will AI/software replace human compliance services?
7. How sticky is the compliance provider relationship?
8. What would you pay more for?

## Validation Targets

Key risks to validate from one-pager:
- Whether software platforms (AgentSync, Agenzee) are replacing human services
- Whether PE-backed consolidation (Saratoga, ReSource Pro) is leaving room for independents
- Whether agencies truly find switching compliance providers painful
- Whether regulatory complexity is increasing or stabilizing
