## Filtering Methodology

- Lead Gen tab: 1,747 total companies. Only 109 had actual dates in the "Date Message Sent" column. Companies with text notes instead of dates were excluded.
- Approved Leads tab: 9 companies with "Message Sent via LinkedIn" status (all art storage). Cross-referenced against Lead Gen to avoid duplicates.
- Referrals/Interested tab: 3 companies (Acumen International, ARTMOVEMENT, Chromatic FAS) that responded and engaged.
- KS Review Status breakdown: 1,010 "To Be Reviewed", 466 "Not Approved (Off Target)", 109 "Approved to Send Initial Msg", 74 "Not Approved (Target too Big)". Only the 109 approved-and-sent were included.
