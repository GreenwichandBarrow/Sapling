# Niche Intelligence Report -- April 14, 2026

## Executive Summary

Four new niches identified through multi-agent convergence scoring: Healthcare Regulatory Compliance SaaS scored highest at 2.70/3.0 (90%), followed by Funeral Home Management Software at 2.68/3.0 (89%), Veterinary Practice Management Software at 2.60/3.0 (87%), and Digital Accessibility Compliance Services at 2.58/3.0 (86%). All four passed the Initial Screen gate and represent "boring vertical SaaS/services with compliance drivers" -- the pattern that consistently scores highest against [[entities/greenwich-and-barrow|G&B]]'s thesis. Healthcare Reg Compliance is the standout as a horizontal platform play across all of Kay's existing healthcare vertical niches.
