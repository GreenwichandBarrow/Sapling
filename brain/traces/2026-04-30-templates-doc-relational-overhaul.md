# V1 BROKERS template overhaul — relational mode, not corporate pitch

## Decision

Broker outreach in the V1 BROKERS variation pivots from a "buyer-with-thesis pitching brokers" voice to a "Kay building a relationship with this specific broker" voice. Buy-box bullets demoted from body to footer reference list. Geography-as-buy-box-criterion stays out of body entirely (authentic personal-touch geography — "your office is near me" — is allowed).

## What was rejected

The 4/29 V1 BROKERS template structure:
- Subject: "Greenwich & Barrow buy-box, would value being on your distribution list"
- Body: holding-company framing + buy-box bullets inline + ask to be added to "buyer distribution list"

Kay's reactions, in sequence:
1. "For brokers — you are looking to set up a call, not just get added to a distribution list."
2. "I just don't like your positioning at all — it sounds like a cookie cutter email, I don't know why they would reply. It should sound more personal, I'm building a relationship with the broker."
3. "At the bottom can be a list of what we are looking for and the fund information, but it doesn't need to be nor should it be in the bulk of an email."
4. "Also I don't think the geography piece should be included in this email outreach."
5. "You are not to define us as a holding company, this paragraph is incorrect."
6. "Just say who I am and that I am looking to acquire and run. Don't mention Greenwich & Barrow. After the introduce my self. The next paragraph can say I have investor backing, and run Greenwich and Barrow as I search full time for the business."
7. "Remove franchise-protected. I don't even know what that is."
8. "You should say my background is in strategy and business development, give something."
9. "I just want to make sure I can get a big salary [→ later: $300K specifically, not a 'big' salary]."

## What landed

Final V1 BROKERS structure:

**Subject:** Brief introduction *(no entity name, no buy-box)*

**Body (3 short paragraphs):**
1. Personal opener + warm-intro variant + Kay's name + background + acquire-and-run intent
2. Investor backing (12 investors incl. industry experts, operators, family offices) + G&B as full-time search
3. Strong-buyer ask — schedule a call + 48hr-NDA-CIM-response credibility signal + flexibility

**Sign-off:** Very best, Kay

**Footer (under signature, Heritage two-block style):**
- "What We Look For" — 4 lines: founder-owned/family-owned US-based, growing recurring revenue, $2-10M EBITDA 15%+ margins, service-driven businesses skilled trades
- "Greenwich & Barrow Snapshot" — 3 lines: founded by Kay Schneider, 12 investors, NY-based

## Why this matters for future agents

Five durable rules emerged from the iteration:

1. **No structural labels in body.** Drop "search fund," "search vehicle," "holding company," "holding company in formation." Don't replace with another structural label — just describe activity in plain verbs.
2. **Lead with personal voice + curiosity about THEM.** The body sounds like Kay reaching out to a specific person, not a corporate buyer addressing a category.
3. **Buy-box info goes to a footer reference list, not body bullets.** Heritage two-block style ("What We Look For" + "Snapshot").
4. **Geography stays out of broker-email body.** Exception: authentic personal-touch geography ("your office is near me") is allowed. Geography-as-buy-box-criterion ("NE preferred") is not.
5. **Strong-buyer pitch in the ask, not list-add language.** "Schedule some time to connect" + "serious buyer with backing in place" + "respond on CIMs under NDA within 48 hours" — signals closure ability without bombast.

## How a future agent should apply

When drafting any intermediary outreach (broker, IB, lender, lawyer, CPA, family office, corporate advisor, association head):
- **Read** `feedback_no_search_fund_language_intermediaries.md` BEFORE drafting — has all 5 rules
- **Pull the canonical template doc** (`G&B Intermediary Outreach Templates`, ID `1_cNsAPCopDAfReoDXbB4d3hZW8TcYUqJ3XKYY_er7i4`) and use the relevant variation as the starting point
- **Keep variations 2-8 in mind** — as of 2026-04-30, V2-V8 still on OLD framing and NEED the same overhaul; pending Kay's per-variation feedback
- **Default to relational, not transactional.** If the draft reads like a corporate pitch, it's wrong.
