### NEW Niche Signals (Not on Any Tracker Tab)

**1. Veterinary Practice Management Software**
- **VHMA** (Veterinary Hospital Managers Assoc): 5,000+ members
- Highly fragmented: ~32,000 vet practices in US, majority independent
- Consolidation wave underway (Mars/VCA, NVA, etc.) but 60%+ still independent
- Software needs: scheduling, billing, inventory, client communication
- G&B fit: operationally critical B2B, recurring revenue, sticky software
- **Signal strength: HIGH** — large fragmented market, active consolidation, software incumbents (Covetrus, eVetPractice) are ripe for vertical SaaS play

**2. Funeral Home Management Software**
- **NFDA**: 19,700 individual members, 10,000+ funeral homes
- **ICCFA**: 7,500+ member companies
- Extremely fragmented: most are family-owned, multi-generational
- Consolidation by SCI/Dignity but vast majority remain independent
- Software needs: case management, accounting, compliance, preneed tracking
- G&B fit: recession-resistant, high switching costs, operationally critical
- **Signal strength: HIGH** — massive fragmented base, multi-generational succession creates acquisition pipeline

**3. Dental Practice Management Software**
- **AIDA** (American Independent Dental Alliance): strengthening independent dentistry
- **AADOM**: dental office managers nationwide
- ~200,000 dental practices in US, ~75% solo/small group
- DSO consolidation wave but independents still dominant
- Incumbent software (Dentrix, Eaglesoft) aging; vertical SaaS opportunity
- **Signal strength: MEDIUM** — large market but DSO consolidation may reduce TAM for independent-focused software

**4. Environmental Remediation Services**
- **ECA**: 50+ members (small, regional)
- **EIA** (Environmental Information Assoc): full vertical spectrum
- Fragmented contractor base, regulatory-driven demand
- **Signal strength: LOW** — small association size, project-based revenue (not recurring)
