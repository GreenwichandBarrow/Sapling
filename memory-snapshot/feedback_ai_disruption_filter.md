Guillermo Lavergne (Ashford Ventures) applies AI disruption risk as a filter when evaluating niches. Fractional CFO was tabled specifically because "too much AI, too much team risk, no searcher fit."

**Why:** Investor buy-in matters. If a niche's core tasks are automatable by AI, investors see it as a risk to the acquisition thesis.

**How to apply:** When scoring new niches, explicitly assess AI disruption risk. Flag niches where the core value proposition could be automated. Software with compliance/regulatory workflows is more defensible than advisory/consulting services.
