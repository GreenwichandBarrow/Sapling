Estate Management was challenged because the end client is a wealthy individual (B2C). Reframed to serve family offices, trust companies, and wealth management firms (B2B) to make it work for DealsX.

**Why:** DealsX's email outreach model targets business owners. B2C framing doesn't produce the right target list or messaging.

**How to apply:** When framing niches for DealsX, always confirm the customer is a business. "Serving family offices" works. "Serving wealthy individuals" does not.
