Investor and advisor call preps should include Insight lines under each bullet — what Kay learned or concluded from the work, not just what happened.

Examples from Kay's Guillermo 4/9 revision:
- "Insight: need to be in front of owners. phone or in person."
- "Insight: best to outsource the volume email & linkedin."
- "Insight: in order to attend this level of conferences need to..."

**Why:** These turn status updates into conversation starters. Guillermo can react to conclusions and push back on thinking, which is more valuable than reviewing a task list.

**How to apply:** For each bullet in a call prep, add a one-line Insight if Kay has drawn a conclusion. Not every bullet needs one — only where there's a genuine takeaway. Also add brief niche descriptions (not just names) and specific financial terms (e.g., "$1,500/mo + $25K success fee" not just "$1,500/mo").
