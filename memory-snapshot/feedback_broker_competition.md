Broker deals have heavy competition. A broker sends an opportunity to their full network of 3000+ emails. When G&B makes offers on these, we are usually not the top bidder and often don't win. This is why investors want 80% proprietary / 20% intermediary.

**Why:** Competitive auction dynamics favor financial buyers who can bid higher. G&B's edge is finding deals nobody else is looking at (proprietary), not winning bidding wars.

**How to apply:**
- Intermediary channel is supplementary, not primary. Never let it crowd out proprietary outreach.
- Track intermediary vs proprietary ratio on the Weekly Tracker. Flag if intermediary exceeds 25%.
- Broker deals are worth pursuing only when: exact thesis match, relationship advantage (warm intro to broker), or limited competition signal.
- Direct broker-to-Kay emails (not blasts) have higher signal — these suggest the broker thought of G&B specifically.
- The Skill Calibration tab should track intermediary deal conversion rate vs proprietary. If intermediary never converts, consider reducing below 20%.
