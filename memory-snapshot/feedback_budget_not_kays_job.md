Kay does not want to manually track the budget or manage books. Period.

**Why:** Her father ran a small business and spent all his growth time on bookkeeping between QuickBooks and Quicken. Kay is determined not to repeat that pattern. She's great at systems thinking and connecting processes, but repetitive tracking tasks are "yuck."

**How to apply:** The budget-manager skill must be fully autonomous. Monthly report should arrive as a "here's where you stand" brief with actionable flags. Kay reviews, never enters data. If something requires manual input, make it a single decision point, not a data entry task.
