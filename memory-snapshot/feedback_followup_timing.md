---
name: Follow-up and thank-you timing
description: Kay sends thank-yous the day after meetings. All follow-ups within 24-48 hours of engagement.
type: feedback
---

Kay likes to send thank-you emails the next day after a meeting. Follow-up on any engagement (meeting, conference booth conversation, call) should happen within 24-48 hours.

**Why:** Timeliness signals professionalism and keeps the connection warm. More than 48 hours and the conversation fades.

**How to apply:** Pipeline-manager should draft thank-you emails the morning after any meeting with a new contact. Outreach-manager's post-conference follow-ups should go out the morning after the conference (T+1). Never batch follow-ups or delay them to "later this week."
