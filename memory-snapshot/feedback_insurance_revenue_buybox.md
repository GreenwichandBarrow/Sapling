Insurance brokerages can have high gross revenue but the relevant metric is commission revenue (a fraction of premiums). $40M revenue for an insurance brokerage is within G&B's buy box.

**Why:** Insurance is valued differently. Don't auto-filter by the standard $2-20M revenue range for this industry.

**How to apply:** When evaluating insurance targets, don't flag high revenue as "above buy box" without understanding the commission structure.
