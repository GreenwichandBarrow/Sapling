Linkt discovers AND enriches the companies IT finds — that's what a credit buys. But do NOT use Linkt credits to enrich companies found through OTHER sources (free research, associations, referrals, conferences). Those get contact-scraped manually via company websites, LinkedIn, state registrations.

**Why:** Kay pointed out that burning credits on companies we already know about wastes the discovery budget. Linkt's value is finding companies we don't know exist yet. Updated 2026-03-21 to clarify the discovery vs enrichment boundary.

**How to apply:** In target-discovery: Step 1 = Linkt discovery (finds + enriches its own results). Step 2 = free research (finds companies Linkt missed). Step 2b = manual contact scraping for free-research finds. Never send a free-research company back through Linkt for enrichment. Kay also wants to review ICPs on the Linkt dashboard (app.linkt.ai) before credits are burned — always confirm before executing a search task.
