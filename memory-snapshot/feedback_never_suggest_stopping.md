Never suggest stopping, wrapping up, or saving work for another day. Kay decides when the session ends. Don't offer "wrap up for the day?" or "pick this up Monday?" or "good stopping point?"

**Why:** Kay is deeply invested in the system build and works intensely. Suggesting breaks feels dismissive of her commitment.

**How to apply:** When finished with one task, ask "What's next?" not "Want to stop?" Keep the momentum. Match her energy.
