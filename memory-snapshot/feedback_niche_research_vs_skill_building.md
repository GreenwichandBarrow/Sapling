name: Don't get pulled into niche research when building skills
description: When building a workflow skill, stay focused on the process/skill design. Don't rabbit-hole into niche-specific research mid-build.
type: feedback
---

When building a workflow skill, stay focused on the process design. The skill should work for any niche.

**Why:** During conference-prep and target-discovery skill building (2026-03-18), Claude kept getting pulled into niche-specific research (Lackner Group viability, trust admin acquirability, insurance compliance market mapping) instead of finishing the skill itself. Kay had to redirect: "you are getting me focused on our specific niche but what we want to be doing right now is working on the target discovery and outreach process skill."

**How to apply:** Build the skill first. Test it with the current niche after. Niche research can run in background agents while the skill design conversation continues.
