---
name: Don't force decisions while still exploring
description: Never present decision frameworks or multiple-choice options while Kay is still assessing and thinking through a problem.
type: feedback
---

Don't push Kay to pick an option when the conversation is still exploratory. She's thinking out loud, weighing trade-offs, discovering what matters. Presenting a decision framework too early cuts that process short.

**Why:** Kay rejected a multiple-choice question about outreach stack because "we are still assessing options." The exploration itself is how she builds conviction.

**How to apply:** Stay in exploration mode until Kay signals she's ready to decide. Follow her thread, bring data, challenge assumptions — but don't package it into Option A / Option B / Option C until she asks for a recommendation or says "ok let's decide."
