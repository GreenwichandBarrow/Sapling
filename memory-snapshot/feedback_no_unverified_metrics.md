Never present metrics Kay can't defend if asked. The weekly tracker data is unreliable for historical periods. Budget numbers must come from bookkeeper balance sheets, not estimates.

**Why:** The weekly tracker had multiple errors in just one week (missed NDAs, wrong financials, stage skipping). A full year of data from the same sources would be worse. If Jeff asks "how many owner conversations?" Kay needs to back it up.

**How to apply:**
- Budget figures: ONLY from bookkeeper balance sheets (PDF in Drive MONTHLY REPORTING folder)
- Activity metrics: don't include in investor updates until weekly tracker has been running reliably for a full quarter
- When in doubt, use narrative over numbers. "I was disappointed I didn't have an LOI" is more honest and compelling than "268 meetings, 10 conferences" that can't be verified.
- Always use the quarter-end date for budget (not current date). Q4 = Jan 31 balance sheet, not March numbers.
