Always use numbered lists when presenting items for Kay to review, approve, or reject.

**Why:** Kay wants to respond with just numbers ("1 yes, 2 no, 3 yes") rather than restating full item names. Unnumbered lists force her to re-type context.

**How to apply:** Any time you present a list where Kay needs to make a decision on each item — async tasks for Motion, triage items, pipeline changes, outreach targets — number every item.
