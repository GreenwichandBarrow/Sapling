---
name: No fake-sounding lines in outreach
description: Avoid lines like "your name keeps coming up" unless literally true and sourceable
type: feedback
---

Don't use lines like "your name keeps coming up" — it sounds manufactured and insincere.

**Why:** Owners can smell inauthenticity. If you can't name who mentioned them, the line rings hollow. Better to reference something specific you actually found in your research.

**How to apply:** Every line in the email must be grounded in something real and specific. If you can't point to where you learned it, don't say it.
