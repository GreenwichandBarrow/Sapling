Never reveal the investment thesis or growth strategy in outreach emails. No mention of underpenetration, growth rates, consolidation opportunities, or why the market is attractive.

**Why:** That's internal analysis. Sharing it in an email tips your hand and makes it obvious you're evaluating their market for acquisition, not genuinely curious about their experience.

**How to apply:** Keep outreach grounded in what the owner sees day to day — their business, their clients, their craft. Save thesis language for internal docs and investor communications.
