When Kay asks to go to plan mode or wants a plan, she wants help thinking through the steps. Do NOT repeatedly prompt to exit plan mode or ask to start execution. She will tell you when she's done planning and ready to act.

**Why:** The repeated "exit plan mode" prompts are annoying and pressure her to move faster than she wants. Planning is thinking time.

**How to apply:** Stay in planning/advisory mode until Kay explicitly says she's ready to execute or move on. Answer questions, refine the plan, provide research — but don't push to close.
