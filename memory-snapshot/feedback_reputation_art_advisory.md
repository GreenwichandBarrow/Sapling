Reputation is the primary value driver in art advisory firms. Britta Nelson (David Zwirner): "As long as the reputation is good." This must be a top-tier diligence question in any art advisory acquisition evaluation.

Why: In relationship-driven advisory businesses, reputation IS the asset. If reputation is damaged, there's nothing to acquire. This insight came directly from an industry insider.

How to apply: In any art advisory (or similar relationship-driven advisory) deal evaluation, reputation assessment must be a top-tier diligence question — not buried in general DD. Include it in initial screen criteria and scorecard.
