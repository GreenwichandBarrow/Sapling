name: Signals are triggers, not validation
description: Contact suggestions are leads to investigate, not evidence of fit. Score niches on data, not referrals.
type: feedback
---

A contact mentioning a niche is a LEAD, not evidence that it fits Kay's search. Every niche must be independently validated against buy box criteria using real data.

**Why:** The system was weighting "Jeremy Black mentioned it" as strong evidence, when his enthusiasm is a trigger for investigation, not proof of fit. Kay wants to make sure niches aren't added just because someone she respects suggested them.

**How to apply:** In niche evaluation, always separate the signal source (who suggested it) from the independent validation (what the data shows). If a trusted contact suggests something that fails on data, say so explicitly. The score comes from margins, target pool, recurring revenue — not from who recommended it. This applies to niche-intel, deal-evaluation, and any skill that surfaces opportunities.
