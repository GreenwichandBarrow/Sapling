Default cold outreach subject line: "Introduction, Greenwich & Barrow"

**Why:** Putting the target's firm name in the subject looks like you researched them to sell to them. It's a cold outreach tell. "Introduction, Greenwich & Barrow" is what Kay naturally used when she wrote to Eric Dreyer and it worked.

**How to apply:** All cold outreach drafts use this subject. Kay adjusts for warm intros or shared interests when reviewing. Never put the target company name in the subject line.
