BK Growth 2025 Searcher Survey (54 funds, Jan 2026): searchers who acquired contacted an average of 4 business owners per day. 72% of funds that acquired contacted 6 or fewer per day. This is the lowest average in survey history (6.4/day overall in 2025, down from 10.1 in 2024).

**Why:** Trend is toward lower volume, more focused outreach. Personalization is table stakes (93% use "some" or more), but volume is declining among successful acquirers.

**How to apply:** Don't push Kay toward high volume (1000/mo). 4-6 personalized contacts/day is the proven model. Quality over quantity. The trade school searcher (4-6/day for 12 months → acquired) aligns with survey data.
