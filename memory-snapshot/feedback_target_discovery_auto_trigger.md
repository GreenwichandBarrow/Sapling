When a niche status changes to "Active-Outreach" (or "Approve - Outreach") on the Industry Research Tracker WEEKLY REVIEW tab, that is the automatic trigger for target-discovery to run. Do not ask Kay for separate confirmation.

**Why:** Kay already approved the niche when she changed the status. Asking again is redundant and slows the pipeline.

**How to apply:** Pipeline-manager and target-discovery should monitor the tracker for status changes. When a niche moves to Active-Outreach, fire target-discovery immediately without surfacing it as a question in the briefing.
