The email cadence (Day 0/3/6/14) and A/B variants (Learning vs Direct) are the **G&B email cadence**, not an Art Advisory-specific cadence. They were designed and approved using Art Advisory as the test case, but they apply to every Salesforge Email niche.

**Why:** Kay built this once to work everywhere. Variables ({niche}, {industry}, {company}, {specific detail}) make the templates niche-agnostic. Don't rebuild per niche — clone and swap variables.

**How to apply:** When creating Salesforge sequences for any niche, use the same master template. When referring to the cadence, call it "G&B email cadence" not "Art Advisory cadence." Google Doc reference: "G&B Email Cadence Templates" in the master templates folder.
