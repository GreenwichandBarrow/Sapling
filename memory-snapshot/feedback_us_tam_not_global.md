When presenting market size or TAM, always use US numbers, not global. Kay operates in the US market. When someone asks for a TAM, it's understood to be US TAM.

**Why:** Mark Gardella draft kept using global $6.6B number. Kay's deck has the US number ($6B). Global numbers aren't defensible in conversation with industry experts who know the US market.

**How to apply:** Any market sizing, one-pager, scorecard, or email draft — default to US TAM. If only global data is available, back into the US share. Reference the niche one-pager/deck for the numbers Kay has already validated.
