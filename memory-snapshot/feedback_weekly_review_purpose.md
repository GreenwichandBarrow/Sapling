WEEKLY REVIEW tab is the working list for the analyst call. It grows as new niches pass the screen. There is no cap on how many can be in WEEKLY REVIEW.

**Rules:**
- Never auto-remove or push out niches from WEEKLY REVIEW to make room for new ones
- Never cap the number of WEEKLY REVIEW items
- Only Kay makes kill/table/advance decisions during the analyst call
- The system ADDS to WEEKLY REVIEW, Kay REMOVES during review

**IDEATION** is the bench — backup niches that only get pulled up if WEEKLY REVIEW items aren't working.

**Why:** Kay clarified that she doesn't want the system managing the WEEKLY REVIEW size. The analyst call is where decisions happen. The system's job is to surface and add, not to curate or remove.

**How to apply:** When niche-intel surfaces a new niche that passes the initial screen, add it to WEEKLY REVIEW. Don't worry about the list getting long — that's a signal of a healthy pipeline. Only put niches in IDEATION if they're truly speculative or have unresolved blockers (e.g., overlap with killed niche needs Kay's ruling).
