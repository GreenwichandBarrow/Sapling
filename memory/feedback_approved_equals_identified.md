---
name: Approved on target sheet = Identified in Attio
description: Col O "Approve" is what triggers a target to enter Attio at Identified stage, not discovery
type: feedback
---

A target becomes "Identified" in Attio when Kay approves it (Col O = "Approve" on the target sheet), not when target-discovery first finds it. Approval qualifies the lead.

**Why:** Kay's approval is the quality gate. Unreviewed Linkt results shouldn't pollute the pipeline.

**How to apply:** Target-discovery creates the row on the sheet. Only after Kay sets Col O = "Approve" does the company get created in Attio Active Deals at "Identified" stage. This changes the outreach-manager and pipeline-manager flows.
