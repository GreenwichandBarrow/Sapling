Art advisory firms are retainer-based (annual/quarterly fees for ongoing collection management, market monitoring, advisory access), NOT project-based. This means art advisory has both fragmentation AND recurring revenue — two of PE's key criteria. Don't characterize the niche as weak on recurring revenue.

**Why:** Kay corrected this directly. The retainer model is a core part of the art advisory thesis and affects how the niche scores on the industry scorecard.

**How to apply:** When discussing art advisory economics, revenue model, or comparing against PE criteria, always note retainer-based recurring revenue as a strength.
