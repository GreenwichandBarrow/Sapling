Calibration workflow should be a weekly skill assessment — not just processing decision traces, but measuring accuracy of every skill and tracking how it changes over time. Col O approve/reject decisions should feed back into targeting criteria at the individual target level, not just niche level.

**Why:** Anacapa article insight — "add a feedback loop to every agent output." Our Col O is a feedback signal we're not systematically using. When Kay rejects a target, capture WHY and refine Apollo search parameters.

**How to apply:** Upgrade calibration-workflow to include per-skill accuracy metrics. Track weekly. Feed target rejections back into target-discovery search criteria. This is the difference between agents that repeat mistakes and agents that learn.
