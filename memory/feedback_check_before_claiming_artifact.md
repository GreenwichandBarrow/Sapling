Don't call something a "SalesFlare artifact" or "no real relationship" without checking. Kay corrected us on Paragon Legal (real outreach happened), Joe McConnell (former boss), Alexis Deller Kushner (husband's recommendation), David Wolkoff (former manager). Every contact is a real person with a real relationship.

Why: Dismissing real relationships as data artifacts is disrespectful and loses valuable connections. Kay's network is built on personal history that may not be captured in CRM data.

How to apply: Before labeling any contact as "no relationship" or "artifact," check Gmail, vault calls, LinkedIn, and ask Kay if uncertain. Default assumption is that the relationship is real until proven otherwise.
