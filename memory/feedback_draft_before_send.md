Always show the draft text to Kay before sending any external message — Slack, email, or any other channel. "Can you write a message" or "is there some language" means prepare a draft for review, not send immediately.

**Why:** Kay needs to review tone, content, and accuracy before anything goes out. Sending without review removes her control.

**How to apply:** For ALL external communications: Slack to JJ, email drafts, webhook posts. Present the draft in conversation first. Only send after explicit approval ("send it", "looks good", "go ahead").
