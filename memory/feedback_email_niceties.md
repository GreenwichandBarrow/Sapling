Always open emails with a nicety before getting into the substance. "Hope this finds you well" or similar.

**Why:** Kay's style is warm and relationship-first. Jumping straight to business feels off-brand.

**How to apply:** Every outbound email draft should have a warm opener after the greeting, before the ask or content.
