When evaluating a niche, **assess it on its own merits using the buy box + filter criteria.** Do not pose "Niche A vs Niche B" framings or "which one should be the anchor" questions during niche discovery.

The goal of niche evaluation is to **continue surfacing niches that fit the buy box.** Multiple niches can pass the filter and be worth pursuing. Comparison + anchor selection happens LATER in a deliberate selection moment, not during exploration.

## Why This Matters

Surfaced 2026-04-19. Claude kept framing F&B Equipment Service as "potentially a stronger anchor than Acumen — does this change the anchor question?" Kay corrected: "please stop weighing one against the other right now - we need to look at niches on their own, for their own metrics. the goal is to continue to surface niches that fit our buy box and criteria."

Comparative framing forces premature elimination of viable niches. It also creates false binaries ("must pick one as anchor") when the strategic reality is multiple good niches can coexist in the HoldCo over time.

## How To Apply

**When a new niche surfaces:**
1. Apply the 3-step filter (segment / mess / AI-resistance) in isolation
2. Estimate target pool (universe of acquirable companies)
3. Check tax treatment (QSBS qualification per `project_frameworks_scorecards`)
4. Discuss growth path (organic + AI professionalization vs roll-up vs both/and sequenced)
5. Discuss customer set, recurring revenue profile, operator profile
6. **Stop there.** Don't ask "vs the others?"

**When the niche passes the filter:**
- Add to tracker as Active-Outreach or WEEKLY REVIEW
- Continue surfacing other candidates
- Multiple passing niches is the desired state

**When time comes to actually pick the anchor:**
- That's a separate, deliberate decision moment Kay will signal
- It uses different criteria (deal availability, capital deployment timing, execution bandwidth)
- It's NOT the niche-evaluation moment

**Anti-patterns to avoid:**
- ❌ "Is this stronger than the existing anchor candidate?"
- ❌ "Should this replace [other niche]?"
- ❌ "Three options for how to sequence this with [other thing]"
- ❌ Comparison tables across niches during niche discovery
- ❌ Bringing the HoldCo anchor question into a single-niche evaluation (REINFORCED 4/19 — Kay corrected this twice in same session)
- ❌ Listing other niches as "anchor-viable vs not anchor-viable" when the discussion is about ONE specific niche
- ❌ Even after a framework correction (e.g., new investor floor introduced), do NOT re-cut other niches against the new framework unless Kay asks. Apply it to the niche under discussion only.

**Correct patterns:**
- ✅ "Here's the filter scorecard for THIS niche"
- ✅ "Target pool is N, QSBS qualifies, roll-up math works because..."
- ✅ "This niche stands on its own — adding to the tracker, continuing surfacing"
