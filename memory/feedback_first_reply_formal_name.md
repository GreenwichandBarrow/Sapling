When drafting Kay's first reply to a new contact, use the **formal first name** even if the counterparty signed casually. Wait for them to explicitly invite the casual form (e.g., "call me Jimmy") before mirroring it.

**Why:** Calibration signal 2026-04-25 from James "Jimmy" Emden (Helmsley Spear) reply edit. Kay changed "Hi Jimmy" → "Hi James" even though his sign-off was lowercase "jimmy." **How to apply:**
- First reply to a new XPX/conference card or warm-intro: use the formal first name from their email signature block, not their sign-off.
- Subsequent replies after they've invited the casual form: switch to casual.
- Senior contacts (Vice Chairman, partner, principal, MD level + 50+ year old based on context) get formal name longer than peer-level contacts.
- Doesn't apply to existing relationships where Kay already uses casual (e.g., she's on first-name terms with Jeff Stevens, Guillermo Lavergne, Saltoun, etc.).
