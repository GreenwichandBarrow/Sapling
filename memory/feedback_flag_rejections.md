When mining call transcripts for niche signals, always read the FULL context of a lead mention. If a lead is proposed but then challenged, shot down, or dismissed later in the same call or a follow-up call, flag the rejection alongside the lead.

**Why:** The overnight deep-dive (2026-03-21) surfaced Hunter's carve-out suggestion as a recommendation, but missed that it was effectively killed in the same conversation (Hunter said banks couldn't underwrite it) and confirmed dead by Guillermo in a separate call. Kay had to catch this manually.

**How to apply:** In the historical and recent call-mining agents, add a step: for each lead surfaced, search the same call AND related calls for contradicting signals. Present leads with their full lifecycle (proposed → explored → outcome), not just the initial mention.
