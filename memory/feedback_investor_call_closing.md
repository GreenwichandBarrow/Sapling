Always end an investor or advisor call by asking them about recent deals or trends they're seeing in the market.

**Why:** Investors and advisors see deal flow Kay doesn't. This is free market intelligence and keeps them thinking about what to send her way.

**How to apply:** Every investor/advisor call prep doc should end with a closing question like "Any deals or trends you're seeing in the market right now?" Include this in meeting-brief-manager templates for investor and advisor call types.
