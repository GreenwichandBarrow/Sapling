JJ-Call-Only routing is reserved for blue collar businesses (trades, field services, physical-world operators — plumbing, HVAC, pest, landscaping, industrial services, etc.).

**Why:** JJ's cold-call playbook works best with blue collar owners who pick up the phone and respond to direct conversation. Luxury, software, and professional service niches require a different approach — email cadence via Kay/DealsX, warm intros, or conferences — not cold calls.

**How to apply:**
- When setting Col D "Outreach Channel" on Industry Research Tracker: JJ-Call-Only gets assigned only to niches that are clearly blue collar
- Luxury / HNW-service niches → Kay Email or DealsX Email
- Software / SaaS niches → DealsX Email
- Professional services (advisory, law, consulting, insurance brokerage) → Kay Email or DealsX Email
- target-discovery and outreach-manager should respect this routing — if a new niche is blue collar, default to JJ; if not, route to email channels
- Ambiguous cases (e.g., specialty insurance brokerage, estate management) → default to Kay/DealsX email, not JJ
