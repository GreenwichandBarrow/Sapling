name: LinkedIn links in draft presentations
description: Always include LinkedIn person + company links when presenting outreach drafts in briefings
type: feedback
---

When presenting outreach email drafts for Kay's review, always include LinkedIn links (person and company) alongside each draft. This lets Kay quickly check their profile before reviewing the email.

**Why:** Kay wants to see who she's emailing at a glance, verify the person looks right, and check connection degree without leaving the conversation.

**How to apply:** Every draft presentation in morning briefings or outreach sessions should show: Name, Company (Variant), LinkedIn person link, LinkedIn company link, then the draft text. If no personal LinkedIn is on file, note it explicitly.
