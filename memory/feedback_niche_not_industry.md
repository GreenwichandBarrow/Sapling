name: Niches must be specific angles, not industries
description: When a niche has distinct sub-segments (domestic vs export TCI), split them into separate niches with separate target lists
type: feedback

Niches are specific angles, not broad industries. When two sub-segments have different customers, carriers, risk profiles, or competitive sets, they are separate niches and need separate target lists, one-pagers, and WEEKLY REVIEW entries.

**Why:** Kay split Trade Credit Insurance into Domestic TCI and Export TCI because they serve different customers (US B2B vs US exporters), involve different carriers (Allianz Trade vs EXIM/FCIA), and have different risk profiles (commercial credit vs geopolitical). Treating them as one niche would blur the target list.

**How to apply:** When target-discovery or niche-intel identifies that a niche has genuinely distinct sub-segments, split them. Each gets its own target list sheet, WEEKLY REVIEW entry, and one-pager. The identifier agent should watch for this during Step 2.
