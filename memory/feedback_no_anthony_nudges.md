When budget-manager is waiting for Anthony's monthly P&L "Management Report" email trigger, do NOT suggest nudging him in the morning briefing or anywhere else.

**Why:** Anthony works on his own cadence. Surfacing "nudge Anthony" makes Kay look needy/micromanaging to the bookkeeper, and it's not Kay's job to chase him. The trigger system fires automatically when his email arrives — that's the right pattern.

**How to apply:**
- When budget-manager status shows "waiting for Anthony's P&L," report the wait silently if at all
- Never propose "Should I draft a nudge to Anthony?" or similar
- If Anthony is genuinely overdue (>2 months past expected), flag it as a system observation, not as a Kay-action recommendation
