No lending businesses. Trade finance lenders, premium finance companies, or any business that lends money or extends credit directly is excluded.

**Why:** Too much liability. Kay confirmed when reviewing ATRAFIN (hybrid TCI broker + trade finance lender).

**How to apply:** When target-discovery finds companies that are hybrids (brokerage + lending), the lending component disqualifies them. Filter to Passed with note in Agent Notes. Applies across all niches.
