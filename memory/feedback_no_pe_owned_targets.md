Never draft outreach to any company that is PE-owned. Hard stop. These are not acquisition targets.

**Why:** G&B acquires independently owned businesses from founders/families. PE-owned companies have different economics, different decision-makers, and different motivations. Reaching out signals Kay doesn't know the market.

**How to apply:** During pre-draft research, check for PE ownership (Venbrook, Parthenon, etc.). If found, flag and skip. This applies to ALL outreach — cold email, conference, warm intro. No exceptions. Add PE ownership check to the enrichment/research step.
