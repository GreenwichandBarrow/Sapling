---
name: Always show people names, not just companies
description: Kay's pipelines track relationships with people, not just companies. Always resolve and display the person's name.
type: feedback
---

Always show the person's name alongside the company name in pipeline views, recommendations, and reports. The relationship is with the person, not the company.

**Why:** Kay said "the people matter in this." Multiple entries for the same company often represent different contacts. Showing only company names makes it impossible to manage relationships and creates false duplicate flags.

**How to apply:** When querying Attio pipelines, always resolve the parent company record AND the associated people/contacts. Display as "Person Name (Company)" not just "Company". When flagging duplicates, show the contact name so Kay can decide if they're real dupes or different relationships.
