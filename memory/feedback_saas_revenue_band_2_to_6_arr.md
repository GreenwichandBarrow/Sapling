SaaS niche size filter: **2-6M ARR**. Not the prior 3-10M band.

**Why:** Sam Singh (DealsX) on 2026-04-15 call confirmed the sweet spot for search-sized SaaS acquisitions is 2-6M ARR. At 10M ARR, SaaS deals at 3-4x revenue = ~$30M acquisition — that's independent-sponsor or PE territory, not searcher-led LBOs. 2M ARR is a valid floor if the business quality is there. Kay approved: "happy to go down the fairway."

**How to apply:** In target-discovery Apollo searches for SaaS niches (DealsX buckets: Enterprise SaaS, Specialty Healthcare SaaS, Female-Led SaaS, Vertical SaaS Luxury), cap at 6M ARR, floor at 2M. Any SaaS target outside this band → flag out-of-buy-box. Non-SaaS niches (services, brokerage) use their own bands and are NOT constrained by this rule. CIO agent owns enforcement.
