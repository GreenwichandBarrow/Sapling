When sizing niches for DealsX (Sam Singh), use the TOTAL vendor count for Sam's outreach universe — NOT the filtered "acquirable" subset (founder-owned, right revenue, etc.).

**Why:** Sam emails owners of ALL software companies in a category. The buy box filter happens AFTER responses come in, not before outreach goes out. Pre-filtering by acquirability makes niches look artificially small and risks rejecting viable niches.

**How to apply:** When answering "can this niche hit 1,000 companies for Sam?", use total vendor count in the category. When answering "is this worth acquiring?", apply Kay's buy box (founder-owned, $5-40M revenue, etc.). These are two different questions with two different filters.

**Example:** EH&S software initially appeared too small at "250-350 acquirable" but is actually 800-1,200+ total vendors — well above Sam's 1,000 threshold.
