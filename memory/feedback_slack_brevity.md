name: Kay prefers brief Slack messages
description: Slack messages to analyst and team should be 2-3 sentences max, not paragraphs with supporting data
type: feedback
---

Kay's Slack messages to her analyst are short and direct — 2-3 sentences, high-level reasoning only. No stats, no fundraising details, no multi-paragraph explanations. The analyst gets the decision and the one-line reason, not the full analysis.

**Why:** Kay rewrote a 5-sentence IPLC kill message down to 3 sentences, cutting all the supporting data (AgentSync valuation, RegEd product launch, zero churn stats). The decision and core reason are enough.

**How to apply:** When drafting Slack messages for Kay's team, lead with the decision, give one clear reason, stop. If they need more detail they'll ask.
