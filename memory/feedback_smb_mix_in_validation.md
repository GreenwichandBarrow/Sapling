Customer validation call lists must include a mix of company sizes. Don't only select Fortune 500 companies. Include small/mid-market businesses (50-500 employees) that would realistically be customers of the independent firms we'd acquire.

Why: G&B acquires independent, owner-operated businesses. Their actual customers are often SMBs, not enterprise giants. Validating only with Fortune 500 gives a skewed picture of the real market.

How to apply: When building customer validation call lists for any niche, ensure a spread of company sizes — include SMBs (50-500 employees) alongside larger companies. The mix should reflect the realistic customer base of the acquisition targets.
