Weekly tracker dashboard tab (10 placeholder questions) is about **process optimization decisions**, not health monitoring.

Two framing lenses for the questions:
1. **Signal Quality** — Are we reaching the right people with the right message? (A/B test results, reply quality, ICP accuracy, warm intro conversion vs cold)
2. **System Throughput** — Is the pipeline flowing or stuck? (targets needed per niche, approval backlog, outreach velocity, conversion between stages)

**Why:** Health-monitor already handles "did it trigger" and "is it broken." The weekly dashboard is where Kay makes adjustment decisions — tighten ICP, switch email variant, refill a niche, pause outreach.

**How to apply:** When adding new questions to the dashboard, they must fit under Signal Quality or System Throughput. If a question is really about system health (tool down, missed trigger), it belongs in health-monitor, not here.
