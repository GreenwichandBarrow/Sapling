Active-Long Term means FINISH what we started — email remaining targets on the list, complete all follow-up cadences (calls, LinkedIn DMs), then stop adding NEW targets. It does NOT mean stop outreach or deprioritize existing targets.

**Why:** Claude kept asking "should we stop?" or "just call the 4 already emailed and let the rest go?" when the niche had 45 targets and only 4 emailed. Wind down means complete the work, not abandon it half-done.

**How to apply:** When a niche is Active-Long Term: (1) email all remaining targets with verified contacts, (2) run JJ follow-up calls on everyone emailed, (3) complete LinkedIn DM cadences, (4) only THEN is the niche truly wound down. No new target discovery, but full execution on existing list.
