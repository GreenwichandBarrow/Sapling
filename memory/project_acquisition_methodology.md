Kay is running a **search fund** through Greenwich & Barrow to acquire a business. Fund runs out **Feb 2027**. Currently mid-March 2026, ~18 months into searching. Pressure is on.

**6-month target:** 1-3 deals under LOI. Ideally 1 under LOI within 2 months (by mid-May 2026).

## 8-Week Business Research Cycle (per niche)

1. **Hypothesis (Week 1)** — Review niches, select 1 activated niche/month, write thesis, define ICP, lock Linkt criteria, go/no-go
2. **Research Design (Week 2)** — Company scorecard, ICP definition, conference mapping (1 target niche + opportunistic), intermediary mapping, owner mapping, run Linkt, define Tier A/B/C
3. **Field Execution (Weeks 3-4+)** — 50 owners contacted, 1 conference/week, direct (75%) + indirect (25%), stage 1 calls, post-conference follow-ups next morning
4. **Data Acquisition** — Financials intake, document completeness check, data normalization
5. **Analysis** — Financial model, terms modeling, company scorecard update
6. **Theory Advancement** — 1-3 LOIs, refined thesis clarity, structured learnings
7. **Model Updating** — Signal quality + system throughput (weekly tracker)

## Tier Framework

- **Tier B:** Next 20-30 — personalized but lower touch, Claude drafts
- **Tier C:** Remaining — JJ cold calls with script

## Investor Survey Benchmarks (2025)
- **4 owners/day** contacted by funds that acquired (vs 9/day for non-acquirers)
- **In-person meeting every 15.6 days** for funds that acquired (vs 36.6 for non-acquirers)
- **Meaningful conversations** matter more than emails sent
- **Response rate** matters more than volume
- **Industry focused = 2x acquisition success rate** (6/34 vs 2/23)
- **5-8 weeks per niche** is the most common focus duration
- **Brokers are universal** — everyone uses them regardless of stage
- **Customized email declining** (63% in 2022 → 24% in 2025), phone and conferences rising

## Key Principles
1. "Acquire momentum, don't manufacture it"
2. Quality over quantity — 4/day not 50
3. Conference = highest value activity for Kay
4. River guides at conferences multiply every conversation
5. 1 interesting deal reviewed per week = solid system
6. Two-week max per hypothesis — kill, advance, or table
7. Need-to-have > nice-to-have (mission-critical, compliance-driven)
8. PE interest = exit validation

**Why:** Solo searcher with fund deadline. Must maximize Kay's strength (in-person) by automating everything else. Previous overseas support failed on quality. Claude replaces the VA role for research/drafting, JJ does the one thing that requires a human voice.
