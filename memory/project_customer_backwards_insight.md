Identified 2026-03-25 from Ruby Au comparative analysis.

**Gap:** G&B goes straight from desk research (niche-intelligence scoring) to owner outreach (target-discovery). Ruby Au validates niches with 10+ customer/expert calls first.

**Why this matters:** G&B's most expensive failures — art storage (balance sheet business), escrow software (no willingness-to-pay), women's health B2B (nascent, VC-backed) — could have been flagged in week 1 with 5 customer calls instead of weeks/months of wasted effort and Linkt credits.

**How to apply:** Proposed "Phase 0" between niche scoring and sprint activation. 5 customer/expert calls via JJ using diagnostic questions from learnings.md. ~1 week per niche. Not a gate — intelligence gathering that runs in parallel with early target-discovery. Pending Kay's review and approval of full plan.
