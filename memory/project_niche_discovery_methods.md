Five niche discovery methods approved for integration into niche-intelligence, ranked by priority:

1. **SBA loan data mining** — Download SBA 7(a) loan dataset from sba.gov, filter by loan size $500K-$5M, group by NAICS code. High loan volume = lots of independently-owned businesses = fragmented industry. Highest signal, most systematic, automatable.

2. **Trade association mining** — ASAE Gateway to Associations (7,400+ orgs). Browse by industry to DISCOVER niches, not just find associations for known niches. Flip the existing approach. Each association = an organized group of businesses = confirmed niche.

3. **Regulation change scanning** — Track new regulations via Wolters Kluwer and Paychex regulatory trackers. Each new regulation creates demand for compliance services. Those service providers are acquisition targets. Feeds the compliance infrastructure thesis directly.

4. **Franchise-as-niche-signal** — FDD Exchange has searchable franchise database. If a franchise exists in a B2B service category, there are thousands of non-franchise independents doing the same thing. The independents are the target pool. Manual but clever.

5. **Boring business database density** — BizBuySell/Acquire.com listing density by industry. High listing volume + low buyer competition = opportunity. High buyer-to-listing ratio = competitive (negative signal). Better as competitive intelligence check than primary discovery.

**Also confirmed (already inline):**
6. Customer-of-target lens — "Who do luxury businesses already pay recurring fees to?" Their vendor lists = niche list.

**PE trend scan** — added as 3rd parallel track in niche-intelligence weekly pipeline (April 2, 2026). Searches PE deal activity, cross-references active/tabled niches, flags PE entry signals.

**Why:** Kay's search fund needs systematic niche discovery beyond personal network, PE reports, and searcher conversations. These methods use public data to surface fragmented industries that match the G&B buy box.

**How to apply:** Integrate into niche-intelligence weekly pipeline. SBA data and trade association mining are the highest-leverage additions — both are data-driven, automatable, and surface niches the searcher community isn't looking at.
