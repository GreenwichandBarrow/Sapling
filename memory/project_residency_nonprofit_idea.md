A wealth strategy contact shared an idea: collectors want their estates to become artist residency programs after they pass. A non-profit could accept donated homes, assess suitability for residency programs, launch qualifying ones, and sell non-qualifying properties to fund operations.

**Why it matters for Kay:** Not a business to run, but a potential board seat / founding role that embeds Kay in the exact community of estate planners, wealth advisors, and collectors who need fine art insurance. Credibility and relationship-building vehicle.

**How to apply:** Back pocket for partner reconnection conversation. Signals how deeply Kay is thinking about the fine art ecosystem. Don't surface as a business opportunity — it's a strategic connector.
