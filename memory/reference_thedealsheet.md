## What TheDealSheet.co is

**Weekly M&A newsletter + website.** They scrape and editorialize deals from BizBuySell, BizQuest, and broker networks — they do NOT originate listings.

## How to use it

**✓ Good use:** industry deep-dives (per-sector pages like `/industries/pest-control/`) — genuinely valuable for market size, multiples, PE platform mapping, owner-demographic signals. Used to cross-reference Premium Pest one-pager on 4/20 and found 1 material correction + 5 missing metrics.

**❌ Bad use:** surfacing TheDealSheet URLs as deal links. The URLs are category/summary pages — clicking doesn't get to the seller. Every "deal" is a re-write of something on BizBuySell/BizQuest.

## Access

Kay has **no subscription** (Gmail verified 4/20). A subscription wouldn't change anything — TheDealSheet doesn't have the listings, the marketplaces do.
