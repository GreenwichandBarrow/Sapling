What We Value

The work behind the work
is trust.

The businesses worth holding tend to share three traits, no matter what industry they are in.

Community

They are part of where their customers live. Quietly, often invisibly, and over a long time.

Service

They notice what their customers care about. The technical work is the cost of admission.

Trust

They have earned the right to be chosen. Customers stay because of the relationship, not the price.

In Other Words

Critical service businesses where the customer chooses on trust.

The work looks operational on the surface. Underneath, the real business is the relationship. The customer cannot choose to go without; they can only choose who provides it.

The owners who have earned that choice for decades, and who are ready to pass it to someone who will steward it the same way: those are the businesses worth holding for the long run.
